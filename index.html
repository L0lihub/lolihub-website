Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our clan

How can I join Lolihub?

Join our Discord server and fill out an application. Our recruitment team will review your application and schedule a tryout match to assess your skills and fit with our community.

What are the requirements to join?

We look for active players with good Skill. While skill is important, we value attitude and willingness to learn. Minimum age requirement is 13, and you must be active in both the game and our Discord community.

Do I need to be highly skilled to join?

Not necessarily! While we have competitive teams, we also welcome casual players who want to improve. Our training programs are designed to help players of all skill levels grow and develop their abilities.

How often does the clan host events?

We host weekly tournaments, monthly clan wars, and daily training sessions. Our event calendar is always packed with activities for members of all playstyles and skill levels.
